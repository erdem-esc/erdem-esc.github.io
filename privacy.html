Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

ESC Games Yazılım A.Ş. ("ESC Games", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile games, applications, websites, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: such as email address or username, if you contact us or create an account.
  • Gameplay Data: progress, achievements, in-game purchases, and interactions.
  • Device & Technical Data: device type, operating system, IP address, language, crash logs, and analytics.
  • Third-Party Data: information from platforms (Google Play, Apple App Store) or linked social accounts.
  • Cookies & Tracking: for our website or ads to enhance user experience and measure performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our games and services
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Enable in-app purchases and verify transactions
  • Monitor gameplay to detect fraud, cheating, or misuse
  • Serve ads and measure ad performance
  • Respond to support requests

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers (cloud hosting, analytics, customer support)
  • Advertising networks to deliver relevant ads
  • Platform operators (Google, Apple) as required for game distribution
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

4. Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is fully secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict use of your personal data. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@esc.gs.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored in countries outside your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate protection of your data.

8. Changes to This Policy
